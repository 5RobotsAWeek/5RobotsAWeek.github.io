October 26, 2020

A team at NASA is working on creating a group of robots -- “cobots” -- that can assemble themselves in a variety of different ways. The design for the individual robots is a sophisticated drone, and its flying abilities plus its ability to work with the other robots allow it to navigate tough and unknown terrain. This team of robots, and the concept, is called Shapeshifter, and researchers hope it can someday be used for missions to Titan, one of Saturn’s moons. Though the concept is still in development -- NASA’s Dragonfly, which is scheduled to launch in 2026, will visit Titan first -- it is a fascinating and futuristic design.
