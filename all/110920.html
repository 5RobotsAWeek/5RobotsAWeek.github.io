This is an example of a self-reconfigurable robot. The basic building block is a “snake” with four degrees of freedom, which can connect to each other via magnets at the ends of the snake. Different attachment patterns allow sets of snake-robots to fold into a variety of shapes, including (but not limited to) bipedal and quadrupedal configurations.
