Boston Dynamics’ robot dog, Spot, is one of the most prominent commercially available quadruped robots. It’s semi-autonomous motion and navigation allow it to easily maneuver uneven terrain, making it an ideal industrial aid. This video, a whimsical clip of Spot dancing to “Uptown Funk'' by Mark Ronson, shows off the robot's vast range of motion. It is also really entertaining to watch a robot dance.
