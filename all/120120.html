Delta robots are a style of robotic arm for which motors are mounted at the top and connect to a lower grabber via motor arms and connecting rods. This makes the moving parts of the robot very lightweight, allowing it to move quickly (the video is not sped up). Because of this, delta robots are common in industrial applications.
